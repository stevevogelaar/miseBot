Global Edit Notes

  • Caption style: White text, top-third of frame, sans-serif (Inter or Roboto), 24px
  • Caption animation: Fade in 0.3s, hold 1.5s, fade out 0.3s (total ~2s per caption)
  • Still duration: 1.5s minimum, 2s for hero/info shots
  • B-roll cuts: Trim to action only — no cursor hovering, no loading dead air
  • Music: Fade in 2s, bed under all shots, fade out 3s at end
  • Export: 1080p, H.264 MP4, under 100MB for Kaggle upload
  • Color grade: Keep natural — no heavy filters. App screenshots should be crisp.
